## Citation Format Notes

All references follow the **APA 7th Edition** format. When citing in-text:

- **Single author:** (Author, Year) - e.g., (Schwarz, 2020)
- **Two authors:** (Author1 & Author2, Year) - e.g., (Chang & Lo, 2021)
- **Three or more authors:** (Author1 et al., Year) - e.g., (Romalee et al., 2023)
- **Organization:** (Organization, Year) - e.g., (WHO, 2022)
